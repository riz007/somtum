Ingredients

A perfect blend of context

🥣

Freshly Shredded Context

Automatically shreds session transcripts into durable observations. Decisions, bugfixes, and architecture patterns are captured fresh, ready to be tossed back into context.

🌶️

Zesty Prompt Cache

Identical or near-identical prompts never hit the model twice. Our fuzzy-embedding cache saves your API credits and keeps your sessions spicy and fast.

🥕

Local Mortar & Pestle

Everything is pounded into a local SQLite WAL database. No cloud accounts, no telemetry, no leaks. Your data stays as private as your secret recipe.

The Blend

Multi-device, Zero friction

Synchronize your memories across machines using simple SSH. Hostname-aware merging ensures your home office and your coffee shop sessions blend perfectly.
